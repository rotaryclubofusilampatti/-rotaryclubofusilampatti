About Us

Founded on the principles of service and fellowship, the Rotary Club of Usilampatti is committed to creating a positive and lasting impact in our community. Our members, united by a shared purpose, work tirelessly to uplift lives through sustainable development, healthcare, education, and environmental stewardship.

As part of Rotary International, we believe in "Service Above Self" and strive to build bridges of understanding, peace, and prosperity. Join us in our journey to make the world a better place—starting right here in Usilampatti.

Our Projects

School Support Program

Visited local schools and distributed notebooks, stationery, and essential supplies to support underprivileged school children and promote education.

Cycle Distribution Drive

Provided bicycles to economically challenged students, ensuring they can travel safely and conveniently to school, reducing dropouts due to distance.
